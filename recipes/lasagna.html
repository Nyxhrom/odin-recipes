Lasagna

Description

Lovely italian dish, you can use different meat sauces or even vegetarian ones (with radish or mushrooms).

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • one portion of bechamel
  • one portion of meat sauce
  • 200g grated parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of water
  2. Cook one lasagna noodle at time for 30-40 seconds and take it out with a skimmer. Put it on a towel to drain
  3. Once finished you can take an oven dish and a spoon. Lasagna, assemble!
  4. Start with a little bit of bechamel and meat sauce at the bottom, spread some parmesan cheese and add lasagna noodle on top.
  5. Repeat step "4" till you finish all the lasagna noodle (at least 5 layers) and finish with bechamel, meat sauce and parmesan cheese on top.

You can either freeze it or cook it at the moment in the oven for 40 minutes at 170Â°.

Enjoy!
