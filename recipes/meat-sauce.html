Meat sauce

Description

Tasty meat sauce, a must recipe to know for an italian cusine enjoyer.

Ingredients

  • 100g Onion
  • 50g Carrots
  • 50g Celery
  • 1 tablespoon Olive oil
  • 300g Ground beef
  • Salt and pepper
  • 100g Red wine
  • 300g Tomato sauce
  • Vegetable stock

Steps

  1. Wash the vegetables and cut in small dices
  2. Put a big pot on the stove and pour the olive oil into it (if you like it you can put a little bit of unsalted butter for more flavour)
  3. Start the fire and put the vegetables in. Let them brown helping you with a wooden spoon for 5-6 minutes.
  4. Add the beef, stir and raise the heat. Let it brown with no rush
  5. Pour the wine and stir till all the alcohol will evaporate
  6. Once evaporated add the tomato sauce. Stir to incorporate and add a couple of cups of stock.
  7. Cover the pot but not fully. lower the heat and let it cook for a couple of hours.
  8. Every 20 minutes or so, check the sauce and add stock if needed
  9. Taste and use salt and pepper to regulate the flavour
