Bechamel

Description

This bechamel sauce will be useful for your lasagna preparation.

Ingredients

  • 1000g milk
  • 80g unsalted butter
  • 100g flour 00
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • a small amount of grated nutmeg

Steps

  1. Pour the milk into a pot. Add nutmeg and salt to it and start the fire
  2. In another pot melt the butter on gentle heat.
  3. Once melted put the flour all at once and start whisking to avoid lumps.
  4. When it's golden you can start pouring a little bit of milk we prepared before. Keep whisking.
  5. After one minute you can pour the rest of the milk and keel cooking the bechamel for 5-6 minutes for a middle density cream, perfect for your lasagna.
